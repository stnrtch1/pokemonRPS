Rock Pokemon Scizor
This Pokemon Rock Paper Scissors based game has two phases, selecting your types and battling.

Selecting Types:

  • First, you select the type(s) you want to use and then confirm them
  • You can choose either one type or two.
  • Consider general strengths and weaknesses when picking your types. Or don't, you're the one playing the game.

Battle Phase:

  • Select one of the five moves and try to do as much damage as possible before the end of the round.
  • Remember to keep an eye on the battle text as you can't see your opponent's types
  • Any types that you have selected will have a Same Type Attack Bonus (STAB) and deal 1.5x as much damage as a normal attack.
  • A round keeps going until the turn count is reached. Once reached, you return to the type selection screen and select new types.
The game keeps going until one player goes down to 0 HP.
